Our Story

— Story Behind Cogmac

Cogmac is a technology based research lab, headquartered in the Noida region of NCR-Delhi. Started with developing new programming languages and cutting edge robotic machines.

— Mission & Vision

Cogmac has the vision to develop low cost robotic solutions which can reach to common people, and help in daily lives. Also, our aim is to help engineers in developing software/hardware solutions much faster by intelligent programming languages, circuit design and machine design. The research division at Cogmac Industries is dedicated to domains such as Healthcare, Food & Beverage, Renewable Energy, Smart Cities, Finance.
