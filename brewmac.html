  • BrewMac

    Blending Taste with Technology

BrewMac is fully automated Cocktail & Mocktail preparing and dispensing machine. It is very easy to install and operate with features like touchscreen, notifications etc. BrewMac caters to the business needs as it can be customized as per business requirements. It has the capability to revolutionize the business of Cocktail & Mocktail industry. It can work like a powerful weapon in the hands of mixologist.

BrewMac helps in managing the Bartending business as it comes with accounting software package. High-end versions of BrewMac has features like Muddling, Blending, Mixing etc.

BrewMac-Features

Fully Automatic AI Based Operations

BrewMac is completely automated solution for all the bartending requirements. Its intelligent AI based processes need minimum intervention form the operator. Once order is placed, BrewMac automatically fetches required amount of liquid/syrups from the bottles.

Touchscreen Functions

BrewMac offers touchscreen which allows bartender to navigate among the different functions with just a touch. Its user friendly interface is easy to use and flexible that provides ease of access with attracting icons.

700 Drinks per Hour

Brewmac is highly efficient machine that could dispense in excess of 700 drinks per hour. Parallel dispensing of liquors/syrups and parallel processing of ingredients makes sure that high number of ingredients in the drink do not reduce the time taken in preparing the drink.

Thousand Types of Cocktails/Mocktails

BrewMac could prepare thousands of cocktails and mocktails depending upon liquors and syrups installed in the machine. Some of the famous Cocktails and Mocktails that BrewMac offers are Mojito, Margarita, Long Island Ice Tea etc.

4 Different Variants

Brewmac is available in 4 different variants that have the capabilities of 10, 20, 30 and 40 bottles of different kinds of liquors and syrups. You could choose the version that best suits your requirements.

ART OF CUSTOMIZATION

BrewMac offers many different customization options. Some of the custom options are:

  • Custom body of the machine suiting the space of your facility
  • Wall mounting option is also available
  • Bottles of liquors and syrups can be set as per requirement.
  • Custom mocktails and cocktails can be created.
  • Why stop at the glass! Brewmac offers Pitcher facility also
  • Space for the number of bottles of liquors and syrups.
IN BUILD SODA PREPARING MECHANISM

With BrewMac, you do not need a separate soda machine. Brewmac has inbuilt Soda Preparing mechanism saving you tons of effort and money

BEST PRECISION IN THE INDUSTRY

Unique dispensing mechanism of BrewMac provides great precision (up to 1ml). High Precision of BrewMac dispensing makes sure that you get the standard taste across all the cocktails and mocktails.

AUTOMATIC BOTTLE LEVEL SENSOR AND NOTIFICATION

BrewMac has sensors that would notify the operator about the critical level of liquor and Syrup Bottles so that they could be replaced in time for the smooth and continuous operations.

ONLINE PROBLEM DETECTION AND RESOLUTION

BrewMac Team provides 24*7 maintenance which is made easy by the Online Problem Detection and Resolution in the software of the Machine.

PLUG AND PLAY

BrewMac has plug and play modules which reduce maintenance time and cost significantly. It also eases customization of machine as per requirements.
